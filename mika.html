The playful one

Mika joined our household in 2022. He's a very active and playful cat - the only thing that he loves doing more than playing with his toys is chasing his sister Athena around the house. He is a furry little fella who needs to be brushed almost daily and enjoys napping under the couch. He is also a very picky eater - Mr. Fancy Pants only accepts dry food in his bowl!

Mika was born in May 2022. He is a black Longhair cat, similar to a Chantilly-Tiffany , with a small white fur spot in his chest and yellow eyes.
