The shy one

Snot joined my previous household in 2009. He was a rescue cat, very afraid of other pets and people, and used to be a pro hiding from everyone. He slowly warmed up to us, but never really got past the "neutral" stage with other pets. He can be described as very poised and regal - can't be bothered asking for attention, will ignore you until he decides you're worthy of petting him - but as soon as it'S food time he's the loudest and messiest eater of them all.

Snot was born in 2008. He is a Tricolor Norwegian Forest cat with yellow-ish/green eyes.
