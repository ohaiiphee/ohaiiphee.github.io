The shy one

Snot joined my previous household in 2009. He was a rescue cat, very afraid of other pets and people, and used to be a pro hiding from everyone. He slowly warmed up to us, but never really got past the "neutral" stage with other pets. He can be described as very poised and regal - can't be bothered asking for attention, will ignore you until he decides you're worthy of petting him - but as soon as it's food time he shows his diva side, loudly asking for food and not leaving you be until he gets fed.

Snot was born in 2008. He is a Tricolor Norwegian Forest cat with yellow-ish/green eyes.
