The oldest one

I first met Misha in 2008 while doing volunteer work at an animal shelter. She had a hard time trusting humans since she had been very mistreated by her former owner. However, after some time, patience and treats, we became inseparable. We would watch tv together, nap together and whenever I sat down at my desk to do some gaming/homework, she would make sure to sit at my lap for moral support (and snuggles). In 2019, Misha became very sick and passed away, leaving us with some sadness, a lot of good memories and a very scratched up red chair (her favorite).

Misha's birth year is unknown. She was a black European Shorthair with yellow eyes. She is dearly missed.
